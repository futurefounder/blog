Launching on Betalist is different from Product Hunt.

With Betalist you send out your application and the team reviews your submission. In case you get approved, you have 3 options:

1: “Funded” Cost $299 USD no waiting queue and get featured as soon as the next day.

2: “Startup” Cost $129 USD no the waiting queue and get featured within the next few days.

3: “Hobby” Cost Free waiting of up to one month. You are not notified when you will be featured exactly.

As I opted for option 3 on the 25th of January 2021.
