Who is the Future Founder and what is this site?

February 8, 2021
  • about

Greetings, my friend from the world wide web.

This website is the result of me failing at publishing online on a regular basis.

I often faced many struggles with (old) new publishing technologies, which got in my way of just writing and publishing.

That is why I have re-thought the way in which I bring things online.

This website makes it easy to just write & publish.

No bloated add ons, ads, extension or other things that get in your and my way.

I am planning to publish as often as possible.

This can be daily, several times per day, or every other week.

If I find a way or structure that suits me, I will let you know right on this spot.
Or, you can join the newsletter-crew

I’m back at doing stuff, or… writing…

Ah, in case you want to contact me it’s

  1. My name (as in the title of this website) written together (no space in between)
  2. The typical sign you use when you reach out to someone over the internet
  3. posteo then a dot as in ‘.’ the me
