So today I will be diving into my problems, my audience and product ideas.

I am noticing I need guidance and a framework again. Like in time constraints and rules I will work by.

So I guess I will try to set my time today to 4 hours focused work on my startup.

Why “only” 4 hours ?

Because I read in Cal Newport’s Book “Deep Work” 4 hours being the optimal time you can work with full concentration on something.

The other time I can roam and work on other things, but this 4 hours must be crucial focused work on the problems I am trying to solve.

Problem Exploration

I will start with myself what are the current problems I am having?

Here is a selection of what I came up with

  • The downsides of being in a cold city
  • I am looking forward to traveling again
  • I regulary miss out on Yoga classes. I lack the regular practice, although there are so many offers and opportunities

5-Why-Process

In order to get to the core of a problem, you can use the 5-Why process.

Let me do it on this one

Why am I not doing Yoga regularly?

Because I do not know which Yoga Class to take.

Why don’t I know which Yoga Class to take ?

Because there are somany options and Yoga styles and I can’t decide.

Why can’t I decide which option to take?

Because I do not want to do something wrong.

Why don’t I want to do something wrong?

Because I think there is a “right class” or a “right practice”.

Why do I think there is a right class?

Because I want to avoid mistakes.

I guess it comes back to comfort zones and breaking habits.

I will try and move forward in the direction of building habits. But, I want something where the entry to doing it is lower for me.

I have started writing on daily basis over the last weeks and have noticed that it significantly helps me structure my mind and get things going.

James Clear talks about starting with really small steps in the beginning of creating a new habit.

It is more important to actually do something that moves you in the direction of your new habit than planning to big goals and not achieving anything.

I have been using templates, but lacked a simple place I can just open and get things going without any distraction.

Yo this will be my starting point.
