Finally started working on my February Micro Startup.

After being done scratching my own itch, market research, audience analysis and talking to people from my audience in IRL over the last 2 weeks I wrote the first lines of code for 🌱📚❤️ VeganBookBase.com.

Took me 3 Minutes and I am currently running through hundreds of Vegan Books, so every minute is valuable.

As my priority is to learn on the go, I am not using a Website Builder on the main page. The Coming Soon page is made via carrd though.

Don’t get me wrong I’m a huge fan of website builders, especially carrd is a favorite of mine and I will continue to use it.

But, they always have limitations and I want to have a better understanding of what is happening under the hood.

So, my aim for this Website is to code this website.

I have created the general structure over the last day.

💡 Audience Insights

First of, the target audience mentioned the great variety of vegan books and the challenges they face when they are trying to find a new one.

Especially people becoming new vegans are having problems with this one.

That is why I want to tackle this problem by creating a website that presents books in a easy to understand way.

If you search for vegan books on search engines many blog articles are information heavy.

Big e-commerce websites like amazon have their own dynamic of presenting popular books, so my angle is to show a selection of curated books in a easy to understand, skimmable manner.

The act of roaming and skimming is known for the target audience from entertainment websites and video on demand services.

“Nothing important should ever be more than two clicks away(..). If something requires a large investment of time—or looks like it will—it’s less likely to be used.” - Steve Krug, Don’t Make Me Think

This is where I am at the moment.

I have the general structure and still have to work on the data, navigation, interactivity and especially mobile friendlyness.
